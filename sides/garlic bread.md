# Garlic bread recipe


## Ingredients

- 4 bread loafs
- 4 cloves garlic
- 2 tablespoon butter
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper


## Instructions

- chop garlic
- bake bread until crisp
- mix garlic, chilli powder, salt, black pepper
- spread butter on breads
- put the mixture on the bread
- bake for few seconds more
- enjoy!
